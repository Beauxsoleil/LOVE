# Your Photos Go Here 💗

Two easy ways to add pictures to the collage:

## Option A — Just use the app (easiest, works on iPhone)
Open the app and tap **"＋ Add Photos"** in the top-right corner.
Pick photos from your phone. They're saved right on your device and show up
in the collage instantly — no files to copy.

## Option B — Drop files in this folder
Put your images in this `images/` folder and name them:

```
1.jpg  2.jpg  3.jpg  ...  12.jpg
```

They'll automatically appear in the collage. `.png` works too — if you use
PNGs, just update the list of filenames near the top of `index.html`
(look for `FOLDER_PHOTOS`).

Any missing slots simply turn into pretty little heart tiles, so the collage
always looks full.
